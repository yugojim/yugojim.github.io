How It Works – Scientific Principle

Amniotic tissues contain a rich source of growth factors, cytokines, and extracellular matrix proteins that promote regeneration, reduce inflammation, and prevent scarring. These components support cellular migration and proliferation, accelerating wound healing in chronic or complex injuries.
