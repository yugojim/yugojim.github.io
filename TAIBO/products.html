Why Amniotic Membrane?

Human amniotic membrane forms the innermost layer of placenta tissue, acting as a protective barrier with non-immunological properties. It provides a scaffold for cell migration and adhesion and is a natural source of cytokines and growth factors.

NeoStim Allografts

NeoStim allografts are dehydrated, terminally irradiated membranes available in multiple configurations and sizes. They are processed in compliance with FDA and AATB standards, ensuring high quality and safety for homologous use.

Derm-Maxx™ Dermal Matrix

Derm-Maxx™ is an acellular human dermis graft sterilized using the Tutoplast Tissue Sterilization Process. It retains the native tissue’s three-dimensional structure, supporting revascularization and the body’s regenerative processes. With a five-year shelf life and simple rehydration, it offers convenience and efficiency.
